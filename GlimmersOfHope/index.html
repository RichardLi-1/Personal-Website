GLIMMERS OF HOPE


Who Are We?

Glimmers of Hope is a youth-led non-profit organization based in Markham, Ontario.

During and after the COVID-19 pandemic, we have found that many youth in our community have been struggling with their mental health, many of which are unable to attend school. In addition, many are unable to access or afford support and many families are uneducated or hold stigmas against mental health. Glimmers of Hope was created to tackle these challenges by providing tailored support to youth in our community.
